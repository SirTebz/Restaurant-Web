Our Food

Thami's Shisanyama and Car Wash when it comes to food is all about the meat and Shisanyama. Shisanyama is a true representation of an eating experience that is genuinely South African! Our menu covers delicious cuts of beef, chicken, mutton, pork and wors. Special requests can be made for your function if confirmed before any event. Pick your meal at the deli, let us weigh it, season it and braai it! While your meat cooks, relax and enjoy a drink. We will serve the braaied meat you requested by our delicious traditional side dishes.

Our food is treated and prepared by an in-house chef. While dining, customer could their cars from strategically positioned out door sits. A free 45 minutes wireless internet connection is being introduced to customers.

Customer retention and satisfaction has always been a focal point with plans for growing the business.
Thami's Shisanyama and Car Wash can now proudly offer the following services:
  • Restaurant - A relaxed atmosphere with an in-house chef preparing the freshest meals.
  • Meat Market - Large Variety of fresh meat products to enjoy at the restaurant or purchase your meat supply for home.
  • Shisa nyama - Purchase fresh meat products to enjoy and have it marinated/braaied onsite in the authentic South African style.
  • Music/Events - During some big events will see Thami's Shisanyama convert to a place of extraordinary life and excitement offering patrons all of the above as well as special events (such as Birthday parties, Fuctions, Events) with the likes of music played by our DJs and Guest DJs on one of the most advanced sound systems.
  • Security - Customers are assured safety with complete CCTV surveillance as well as Security patrollers.
