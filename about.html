About us

Mission

In Thami's Food and CarWash we are guided by relentless focus on our imperatives, we will constantly strive to implement the critical initiatives required to achieve our vision and goal.

Vision

The profitable growth through superior customer service, innovation, quality and commitment.

Values

Our values do not drive the business, they drive the people within the business. Values at Thami's Shisanyama and Car Wash are internalized by the people in our organization to have meaning:

  • Best People: Attract, develop and retain the best for our business, challenging our staff, demonstrate a "can-do" attitude and foster a collaborative and mutually supportive environment.
  • Client Value: Enables clients to enjoy quality experiences which we offer and creating long term relationships by being responsive and relevant and by consistently delivering value.
  • Integrity: Being ethically unyielding and honest and inspiring trust by saying what we mean, matching our behaviours to words and taking responsiblity for our actions.
